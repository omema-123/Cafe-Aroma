About Café Aroma

Started as a cozy corner for friends and family, Café Aroma serves hand-picked beans and freshly baked treats. Our goal is simple: a warm space with honest flavors.

  • ☕ Locally roasted beans
  • 🥐 Fresh bakes every morning
  • 💻 Free Wi-Fi & comfy seating
